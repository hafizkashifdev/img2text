Tips to Convert PDF to the Text file

Technology can sometimes jolt you out of the blue. Imagine you notice errors in your PDF report or assignment that you are going to submit. You may immediately become anxious because you need to submit by the specified deadline. At this point, everything seems out of whack.

But there is a way out. Get an image to text converter, scan your pages containing errors and edit them instantly!

It is essential to define PDF before moving towards our answer.

Definition of PDF: As defined by Tech Terms, "It stands for Portable Document Format. PDF is a file format designed to present documents consistently across multiple devices and platforms. It was developed by Adobe in 1992 and has since become one of the most widely used formats for saving and exchanging documents".

Before the genesis of PDF, tech geeks were looking for a file format that could be compatible with almost every intelligent electronic device and easy to use. To work on this idea, Mr. Warnock decided to develop it initially for its organizational use.

He once said," Imagine being able to send full text and graphics documents (newspapers, magazine articles, technical manuals, etc.) over electronic mail distribution networks. These documents could be viewed on any machine, and any selected document could be printed locally. This capability would truly change the way information is managed".

The idea was relatively novel because it streamlined the use of a single file format across the globe. After all, almost all Windows and iOS users are using it today.

How can PDF file not be an easy-to-fit format?

But that does not mean this format of representing text fits in every scenario. Technology has both advantages and drawbacks, and they evolve with time. The same is the case with Portable Disk Format. Let me show you a few reasons that show this format is not the perfect one. Inefficient for screen: As I have discussed above, it was designed for easy distribution of documents across networks compared to fax. Therefore, a person has to print a PDF file once it is received. It is very compatible with paper-when printed; your text fits the paper.

But the drawback of PDF is that it cannot be used as a better option for on-screen reading. A casual reader cannot easily navigate through PDF files to get the desired information. It also reduces the reader's attention span as content is distributed across the screen.

Content is not changeable:

The biggest drawback of PDF is that we cannot edit our content. Human errors and typos are pervasive in texts and can be discarded, but PDF format does not give you that freedom. Furthermore, once you have converted your file into PDF, you cannot change it unless you use a specific tool.

For the above reasons, you need an image-to-text converter for a better experience of editing and on-screen reading.
